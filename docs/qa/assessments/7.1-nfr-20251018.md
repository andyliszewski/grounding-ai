  - "Mere aggregation" exception documented (licensing-summary.md:36-68)

  - No import/linking → No copyleft obligations triggered
  - Source disclosure requirement met (Audiveris already on GitHub)

- ✅ **BSD 3-Clause analysis (py4j, music21):**
  - Permissive license, no copyleft (licensing-summary.md:82-123)

  - Modifying Audiveris: Triggers full AGPL obligations (licensing-summary.md:244-257)
  - Commercial deployment: Approved with subprocess pattern (licensing-summary.md:259-274)

- ✅ **Attribution requirements:**
  - BSD license notices documented (licensing-summary.md:185-207)
  - Compliance checklist provided (licensing-summary.md:210-222)

- ✅ **Fallback plan:**
  - homr (MIT) available if AGPL becomes blocker

**Assessment:** ✅ **PASS** - Licensing analysis is thorough, legally sound, and commercial-friendly.
