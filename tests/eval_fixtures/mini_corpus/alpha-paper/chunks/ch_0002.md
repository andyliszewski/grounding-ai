section_heading: 2. Quantum Field Theory
---

Quantum field theory generalizes quantum mechanics to relativistic systems
and underlies the Standard Model of particle physics. Renormalization is the
set of techniques used to remove divergences from loop integrals, producing
finite predictions for observable quantities. Lattice formulations discretize
spacetime and let numerical simulations probe non-perturbative regimes.
