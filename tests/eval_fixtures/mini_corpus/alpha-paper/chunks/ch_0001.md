Quantum mechanics describes the behavior of matter and energy at atomic
and subatomic scales. The transformer neural network architecture, introduced
by Vaswani and colleagues, relies on multi-head self-attention to model
long-range dependencies in sequences. Recent work explores applying transformer
attention mechanisms to simulate quantum systems, where tokens represent
basis states and attention weights encode superposition amplitudes. This
topic sits at the intersection of physics and deep learning.
